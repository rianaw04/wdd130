Welcome to Our White Water Rafting Adventures!

This page provides detailed information and examples of the different adventures or trips that the company offers, including available equipment.

Trip 1: The Rapids Challenge

Experience the thrill of navigating through Class III and IV rapids. Perfect for adventure seekers looking for a challenge.

Available Equipment:

  • Helmet
  • Life Jacket
  • Paddle
  • Wetsuit

Trip 2: Family Adventure

Enjoy a scenic and fun trip down calmer waters, ideal for families and beginners. Suitable for children aged 6 and above.

Available Equipment:

  • Helmet
  • Life Jacket
  • Paddle

Trip 3: The Explorer's Journey

Take a multi-day rafting trip and explore the beauty of nature. Camp under the stars and enjoy meals by the river.

Available Equipment:

  • Helmet
  • Life Jacket
  • Paddle
  • Tent
  • Sleeping Bag
  • Cooking Gear
