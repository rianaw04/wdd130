Antigua and Barbuda

Antigua and Barbuda is home of 365 beaches, one for every day of the year meanwhile Barbuda is filled with Pink Sand beaches. Both islands combine are a match made in Heaven. The islands are festive all year round but even more so during the summer, this is when the greatest summer fest is celebrated: Carnival.
